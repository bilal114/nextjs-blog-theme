For information about your ability to restrict the collection and use of Geolocation Information to enhance our Services, please refer to the settings available in your device.

To record the safety measurements of your driving (accelerating, cornering, speed, braking) our mobile applications may need to access the activity recognition functionality of your mobile phones operating system. If you do not agree to our collection of Motion Detection from within your device settings, our Services may not function properly when you try to use them and may not be able to assess your record as a safe driver.
  • Call & SMS information that is not mandatory for you to provide in order to use our mobile applications. As a safety feature, our mobile applications offer the functionality to turn on automatic call and SMS replies for incoming calls and SMS that are received whilst our mobile applications are being used to record a driving trip. This enables you to drive without interruption and improve your safety. It is optional for you to use this function. If, however, you choose to use it, it is necessary for you to permit access to the SMS and call functions through the permission system used by your mobile phone platform. We will only utilize these functions for the stated purposes.
  • Car Make/Car Model/Car Year/Vehicle Registration Number information that is not mandatory for you to provide in order to use our mobile applications. For the purposes of rewarding you for being a safe driver, we may collect this information to ensure our mobile applications can assess your driving behavior more accurately.

  • Other Information in such cases we always transparently ask you to provide your information at your disposal and we always define the purposes of using that information in advance (e.g., name of the company you work for, your job title or position or information about your dependents etc.)
